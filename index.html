Lesson 1: Variables & Print Magic

What are Variables?

Variables are mystical containers that store your data like treasure chests! Think of them as labeled magic boxes where you can keep numbers, text, or any information you need in your legendary programs.

The Legendary Print Function

The print() function is your cosmic megaphone to the universe! It displays output on the screen, broadcasting your results, messages, and helping you debug like a Python wizard.
